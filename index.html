Total Health Care Solutions

At Helpful Hearts, we believe in providing comprehensive health care solutions tailored to meet your individual needs. Our range of services ensures that you have access to the latest in medical diagnostics and wellness. Your well-being is our priority, and we're here to offer you a seamless healthcare experience.

Our Medical Specialists

Meet the dedicated team of medical professionals at Helpful Hearts, committed to providing expert care in various specialties. Each specialist brings a wealth of experience and expertise to ensure you receive the highest standard of healthcare.

Dr. Austin Distel

Dr. Austin Distel specializes in mental health and emotional well-being. With a compassionate approach, he helps individuals navigate challenges such as anxiety, depression, and stress, providing personalized treatment plans to promote mental wellness.

Dr. Tin Bern

Dr. Tin Bern, our experienced dentist, is dedicated to ensuring your oral health. From routine checkups to advanced dental procedures, she focuses on creating healthy smiles and providing comprehensive dental care for patients of all ages.

Dr. Bruno Rodrigues

Dr. Bruno Rodrigues, our esteemed cardiologist, specializes in heart health. With a focus on preventive care and advanced diagnostics, he is committed to helping you maintain a healthy heart and addressing cardiovascular concerns.

Dr. Dalton Ngangi

Dr. Dalton Ngangi, our skilled neurosurgeon, brings expertise in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological conditions. From spine surgeries to complex procedures, he is dedicated to providing comprehensive care for neurological well-being.

Dr. Humberto Chavez

Dr. Humberto Chavez, our orthopedic surgeon, specializes in musculoskeletal health. Whether it's joint issues or orthopedic injuries, she offers surgical and non-surgical interventions to restore mobility and improve overall orthopedic health.

Dr. Usman Yousaf

Dr. Usman Yousaf, our pulmonologist, focuses on respiratory health. With expertise in treating conditions like asthma, COPD, and lung diseases, he strives to enhance your respiratory well-being through personalized care and advanced treatments.
